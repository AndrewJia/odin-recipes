Ingredients

  • 1 cup Oat Flour
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1/2 cup creamy Peanut Butter
  • 1/4 cup Maple Syrup
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tbsp melted and cooled Coconut Oil
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1/3 cup Cacao Powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees, and grease or line with parchment paper a
    baking pan.
  2. In a medium bowl whisk together the wet ingredients - creamy peanut butter,
    maple syrup, egg, coconut oil, and vanilla.
  3. Then separate the wet ingredients into two bowls. In one of the bowls, mix
    in the cacao powder.
  4. In another medium bowl, combine the oat flour and baking powder.
  5. Take 1/2 cup of the dry flour mixture and add it the cacao wet mixture,
    and take the other 1/2 cup of dry flour mixture and add it to the peanut
    butter mixture.
  6. Wet hands slightly, measure out 1-2 tsp of mixture, and roll into a ball.
    Place on baking tray.
  7. Roll all the wet mixture (PB and Cacao) into balls and place on the baking
    tray. Bake for 12-15 minutes.
  8. Best served in a bowl, with almond milk!
